What does 'IBH' stand for?

IBH stands for Insert Buzzword Hear, so our legal name is Insert Buzzword Here Technologies

Why Insert Buzzword Here?

As people who've watched the tech scene grow, and after following a plethora of startups, we discovered we didn't like what we saw.
Just like "Synergy", and "Scalable", more often than not startups have terribly buzzy names.
We wanted to avoid this.
We want to stay true to what we are
We're here to serve, not to create buzz

What's with the 'Black and White' motto?

In short:
For reasons similar to the IBH choice
In full:
We want to get you what you want
We want to keep that simple
We want to keep that efficient
We believe that's the epitome of being 'Black and White'
